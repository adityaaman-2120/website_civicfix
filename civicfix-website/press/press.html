Media FAQ

Rapid answers for journalists on tight deadlines.

What is the primary goal of CivicFix?
To eliminate the friction between citizens and authorities by providing a transparent, tech-enabled platform for reporting and resolving civic issues.
How does CivicFix ensure data accuracy?
We use AI-based photo verification and geo-tagging to ensure every report is legitimate and precisely located before routing it to authorities.
Is CivicFix available for public-private partnerships?
Yes. We actively collaborate with municipal bodies and smart city departments to optimize their response workflows.
