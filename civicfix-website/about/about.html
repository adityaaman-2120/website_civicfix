Who We Are

Problem-Solving

CivicFix is a civic technology platform designed to simplify the way citizens interact with local authorities. We aim to bridge the gap between people and governance by providing a digital solution for reporting and resolving everyday civic issues.

Our Genesis

CivicFix was born out of a simple yet powerful idea—to make cities more responsive and citizen-friendly. Observing unresolved complaints and lack of communication, we realized the need for a smarter solution. What started as a spark has grown into a platform empowering millions.
