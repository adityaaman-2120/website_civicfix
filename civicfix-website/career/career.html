Who We Are

Empowering Citizens,

At CivicFix, we are building a platform that empowers citizens to report, track, and resolve civic issues in real time. Our goal is to create cleaner, safer, and more efficient cities by bridging the gap between people and authorities.

Working at CivicFix means being part of a fast-growing, innovative environment where your ideas matter and your work directly contributes to improving urban life.
